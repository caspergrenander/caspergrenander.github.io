Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access the C / A Beats Publisher system? +
Access is restricted to authorized personnel within C / A Beats & Productions. All users must have valid Meta Platform access tokens and be approved by system administrators.
How do I request data deletion? +
Use our Data Deletion Request form or email us directly. Data deletion requests are processed within 7-10 business days.
What are the system's operating hours? +
The system operates 24/7 for scheduled publishing. Technical support is available during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM, Monday-Friday).
How do I report a technical issue? +
Use the contact form above and select "Technical Issue" as the subject. Include as much detail as possible about the problem, including error messages and steps to reproduce.
Is my data secure when using this system? +
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use OAuth 2.0 authentication, and access tokens are securely stored. Regular security audits are performed to maintain system integrity.
